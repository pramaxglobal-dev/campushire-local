> **Authoritative remediation update — 2026-07-13:** The findings below are the pre-remediation evidence set. The following audited gaps are now resolved and must not be treated as open: tenant CRUD/status (`apps/web/src/app/(dashboard)/dashboard/admin/management/page.tsx:68-177`); platform settings (`:191-197`); broadcast (`:199-202`); job moderation (`:181-188`); vendor progress/edit/rating (`apps/web/src/app/(dashboard)/dashboard/vendor/page.tsx:102-110`, `apps/web/src/app/(dashboard)/dashboard/vendors/page.tsx:142-196`); course edit (`apps/web/src/app/(dashboard)/dashboard/training/page.tsx:128-179`); Training Partner course chat (`:145-155`, `apps/api/src/modules/chat/chat.service.ts:177-193`); real account deactivation (`apps/web/src/app/(dashboard)/settings/page.tsx:302-309`, `apps/api/src/modules/users/users.service.ts:358-410`); persisted tagline/subdomain and tenant selection (`apps/web/src/app/(dashboard)/dashboard/whitelabel/page.tsx:108-151`, `apps/api/src/modules/whitelabel/whitelabel.service.ts:137-179`); mobile application/job details and role workspaces (`apps/mobile/src/app/applications/[id].tsx:11`, `apps/mobile/src/app/jobs/[id].tsx:12`, `apps/mobile/src/app/(tabs)/role.tsx:25`); event edit (`apps/web/src/app/(dashboard)/dashboard/events/page.tsx:111-145`); and interview reschedule/cancel (`apps/web/src/app/(dashboard)/dashboard/interviews/page.tsx:113-153`). - `rg --files apps/mobile/src/app -g '*.tsx'` found **10** mobile route components.
- Every reported API helper was checked against the 20 Express route files in `audit/SECURITY_CHECKLIST.md`; database conclusions were checked against `audit/DATABASE_AUDIT.md` and the cited service operation.
- Fully functioning controls are intentionally absent from this exception report.
